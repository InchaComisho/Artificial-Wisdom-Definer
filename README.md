## Difference Between AI and AW

Artificial Intelligence asks:

```text
Can this be done?
```

Artificial Wisdom asks:

```text
Should this be done?
Under what limits?
What long-term consequences may follow?
Can humans supervise it?
Is it reversible?
Does it preserve ecological and social continuity?
```

AI expands capability.

AW tunes the use of capability.

AI can increase speed and scale.

AW checks whether speed and scale are aligned with long-term stability, natural law, accountability, and regeneration.
